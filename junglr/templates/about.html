About Junglr

As a jungler, I always wanted to know what impact I was having in a game and this project is an attempt to quantify that. I hope to answer questions such as "What actions will increase my team's chances of winning? What objectives should I prioritize?" If you have any suggestions for features, please email me at admin(at)junglr(dot)com. Thanks!
